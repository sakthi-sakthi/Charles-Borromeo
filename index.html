Our Crest

The whole crest is surrounded by lilies and violets in the midst of which is the motto: LABOUR, FAITH, and PROGRESS Education in all Our Ministries.

Our Charism

To Incarnate the Compassionate Love of God for all people especially the poor and the Marginalised through Christian Education in all Our Ministries.
